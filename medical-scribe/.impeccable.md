## Design Context

### Users

The primary user is a clinician reviewing documentation immediately after a patient encounter. For the MVP, the live audience is hackathon judges watching a synthetic encounter move through the complete offline pipeline.

### Brand Personality

Calm, clinical, and careful. The product should create confidence through transparency and restraint rather than futuristic AI spectacle.

### Aesthetic Direction

A light, utilitarian clinical workspace inspired by a well-designed paper chart: warm tinted surfaces, strong text hierarchy, generous whitespace, and one restrained green action color. The interface must never resemble a glowing AI dashboard or a dense hospital administration portal.

### Design Principles

1. Make the next action unmistakable.
2. Show what the local models are doing in plain language.
3. Keep transcript evidence beside the editable draft.
4. Treat every generated note as unverified until reviewed.
5. Prefer recovery paths and useful errors over additional features.
